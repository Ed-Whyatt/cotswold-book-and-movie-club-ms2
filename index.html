About Us

We are a small group of volunteers who simply love the book and movie scene. Our mission is to bring likeminded people together and share our own stories at the same time. We go out of our way to find good content for our club.
Here you will find information on where our meets are and what we will be doing. Feel free to use our site to search for a book or movie that you just can't remember when is was made or who the author is.
